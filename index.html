// about me

My Story

2017 – 2022

Assembly & C++ at Wits

Discovered programming through Software Development, Data Structures and Algorithms & Microprocessors at the University of the Witwatersrand. Built board games in the terminal, a Centipede clone with SFML, and fell in love with the control that low-level languages give you. This is where I developed my obsession for clean & performant code, DRY principles (modularity & maintainability), and Git (version control - used right).

2023

Flutter & Mobile Dev at PLP

Joined the Power Learn Project bootcamp, specialised in Flutter, and built GovTrackSA — a mobile app prototype aimed at making government information easily accessible. I had always loved beautifully designed apps; Flutter gave me the power to create them.

2024 – Present

Real Production Code at Siyakha

Started as an intern in May 2024 — thrown straight into Laravel with minimal web development experience. By December, I was offered a full-time role — the only one out of six interns.

In under a year I went from building a basic contact form to leading the development of CEP© — a platform Siyakha used to partner with Coxx Energy and local leadership in Bela Bela, Limpopo, listing job opportunities for an upcoming Solar PV project to drive real sign-ups. Over the campaign period, 241 job seekers and 19 organisations from the community registered on the platform.

Since joining full-time, I’ve maintained and expanded features across CEP©, yowza!©, and #SpazasConnect©, managing deployments, troubleshooting production issues, and ensuring updates follow proper test-to-production workflows (never direct changes in production).

"I want to be a master of my craft. You know when they speak about the 10,000 hours? Yes, that. I am just at the beginning."
